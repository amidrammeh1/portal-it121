About Me

Aminata Drammeh who goes by Ami is an empowered young Black-Muslim professional on the pathway to developing her personal and technical skills.

Tutoring for a non-profit while embarking on a journey with Year Up's Software Development Track. Her colleagues would describe her as a determined young mother ready to make her mark.

This journey has put me on a pathway to connect with others and grow within the technology community.

Being a mom, I am motivated and determined to be my ancestors wildest dreams and use my innovative and problem solving skills to make an impact in my community.

“Trust in yourself, believe that your voice matters, and know that your words are good enough.” - Amber Gordon. I believe in the value I will add to the tech community while always staying true to who I am.

Goals and Aspirations

Being a problem solver and creative are things I highly enjoy. I would love to use these skills to become an experienced Software Developer coding away at real world problems and adding ME to the world.
